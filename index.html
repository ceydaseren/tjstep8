Step 8 Activation Phase

Activate Your Energy

Connect with your higher self, soul, and spirit team. Declare your chosen timeline and call forward divine support to anchor it into this reality.

The Teaching

You have completed the Selection phase. You know what you do not want, you have chosen what you do want, and you have named it. Now you are going to align yourself energetically and declare it to the forces that are already trying to support you.

Whether you fully believe it or not, you have guides and higher aspects of yourself that are constantly working on your behalf. Many times, unless you ask for help, they cannot intervene. That is part of the law of free will for being here on Earth. This step is your formal request. Through your declaration and your asking, you are calling forward their assistance.

What you are doing here is having an intentional conversation with your higher self, soul, and spirit team. You are telling them what you want, stating the timeline you have selected, and requesting their support in elevating your frequency so that you can begin living it. This can be a quick spoken declaration, a longer meditation, or a written letter.

Before You Begin

Close your eyes and take three slow, deep breaths. Request that your higher self, soul, and spirit team step forward and hear you now. Bring your timeline name from Step 6 to mind as you will be using it in the declaration below. When you feel settled, open your eyes and begin. You can speak the words out loud, write them by hand, or simply read through the script and let the intention land. There is also a guided meditation linked at the bottom of this step if you would like support.

Your Declaration
Higher Self, Soul & Spirit Team
Through time and space, I call forward my higher self, soul and spirit team who is serving the light only and loves me unconditionally only to step forward.

I am asking for divine interference in the most enjoyable way to anchor myself into this new timeline of [your timeline name].

[Feel free to elaborate on what your new reality will look like.]

I command this to be true in this life and reality through my divine will and God's spark within. It is done, it is done, it is done.
Speak this out loud or write it by hand. Say it with conviction. The words "it is done" are a declaration of certainty, not a wish.
